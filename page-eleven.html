Christmas Memories

Always on-the-go and on-the-job, Angelababy’s schedule is packed with work commitments — even during holiday periods such as Christmas and New Year’s. “I do remember one year, when I was alone in Japan,” she said, “and I cried because I felt so lonely to be on my own at Christmas.” The next year, she was on set for a movie, so happily shared a turkey gift she had received. “At that moment, I knew that Christmas should be about warm, happy feelings, and sharing beautiful moments with loved ones.”

Her dream Christmas is to celebrate the Western festival in its purest form. “I would love to visit Europe during this time to really experience it in a more traditional sense,” she explained. “From a home-cooked Christmas dinner to beautiful snow scenes, honouring family traditions and old legends, I think it would be one of the most memorable holidays ever.”

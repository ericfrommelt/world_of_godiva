Regal Dreams

As for her future, Angelababy still dreams like a princess — and hopes to eventually marry her prince one day. But, her wedding would not be a typical royal fairy-tale one. “My dream wedding is totally different to what people would expect,” she said. “If it were to be a wedding scene in a movie, it would be in a gorgeous, grandiose, castle-like place where I’d be crowned a queen. However, I want something much simpler.”

Cherishing Love

“I really would love a perfect dream wedding one day, that’s simple yet romantic and warm, just for my family and close friends,” Baby said. “As a celebrity, I have to face and work with people and press every single day. So I hope that I can keep my marriage and wedding private, as something personal and kept between my love and closest ones, so we can cherish the experience together.”

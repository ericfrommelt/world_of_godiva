Divine Dining

Calling all chocolate lovers: Dinner is served

Highlights from the dinner

To commemorate its 2013 Brussels tour with Hong Kong celebrity Angelababy and key Asian media from China, Hong Kong and Taiwan, GODIVA hosted a private dinner at the two-storey Maison Grand Place — a classic, elegant historical mansion located in the heart of the city.

With welcome canapés created by GODIVA Executive Chef Chocolatier, Thierry Muret, and Chef Chocolatier for Pacific Rim and China, Philippe Daue, followed by an incredible eight-course menu specially tailored by guest chef Fabian Beeckman and GODIVA in-house chefs.

Chef Beeckman sourced the finest meats and seafood, plus the best local produce. He worked with the GODIVA chefs to merge their rich, fresh tastes with the best of GODIVA chocolate, for four of the six exquisite dishes. The results were gastronomically divine and superbly delicious.

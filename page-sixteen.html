Truffe Praliné Brésilienne

Our signature hazelnut praliné using perfectly roasted hazelnuts and a touch of vanilla, coated in smooth milk chocolate and rolled in crispy caramelized hazelnuts – a rich taste to remember.

Truffe Mousse Matcha

A truly inspired Godiva Original: exotic Matcha tea mousse, covered in milk and dark chocolate, then rolled in Japanese Matcha powder.

Truffe Praliné Amande

The nutty caramel accents of our 43% Venezuelan cocoa milk chocolate Praliné blend naturally with almond and feuilletine, all surrounded in milk and dark chocolate – a taste sensation.

Truffe Mousse de Lait

This special milk chocolate mousse derives its nutty hints from our 43% Venezuelan cocoa chocolate surrounded by milk chocolate and rolled in fine cocoa powder – sheer delight.

Truffe Speculoos

Inspired by the traditional Belgian Biscuit Speculoos. A light yet very sophisticated mousse, with a hint of speculoos surrounded by smooth milk chocolate and covered with a Speculoos biscuit crunch – a truly Belgian treat.

Truffe Mousse Vanille Miel

The complex Wildflower honey enhances a ganache of white chocolate combined with light vanilla mousse. All covered in dark chocolate and topped with creamy white chocolate flakes – a magical experience.

Truffe Caramel Salé

An intriguing blend of rich liquid caramel and sel de Guérande, covered in both milk and dark chocolate and topped with dark chocolate slivers.

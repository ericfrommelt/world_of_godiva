Lady Legend

Known for her selfless devotion, Lady Godiva dedicated her life to the impoverished and downtrodden. Married to the powerful Lord Leofric, who imposed heavy tax upon his subjects, Lady Godiva protested his unkind ways.

Leofric offered that, if his wife rode through the streets of Coventry “clad in naught but her long tresses” and if the town’s folk remained indoors, resisting a glimpse of the ultimate temptation, he would lift the taxes.

The following morning, Lady Godiva made her famous ride, and the citizens graciously remained indoor. Leofric kept his word, and the legend of Lady Godiva became a mythical tale of passion, boldness, style and sensuality — that went on to inspire GODIVA founder Pierre Draps.

Lunar New Year

GODIVA is delighted to pay tribute to the 2014 Chinese horoscope creature: the majestic horse. Named in honour of the legendary Lady Godiva, who famously rode naked through Coventry on a horse, GODIVA’s Limited Edition Year of the Horse collection includes iconic prosperous-red gift boxes of Lady Godiva-shaped chocolates.

New flavours include: milk, dark, and white chocolates; with the carré gift box offering bites of crunch milk and dark strawberry chocolate squares.

Valentine’s Day

Lovers and deep romantics can turn to GODIVA and French artist Nathalie Lete for heart-felt inspiration. Offering an extensive collection that includes new chocolates of desire, heart-shaped delicacies and an impressive romantic gifting range, Lete’s beautiful love-inspired graphics add a collectible, creative touch.

“For me, Valentine’s Day is about appreciating and sharing, as well as generosity and sweetness,” Lete says. “I envisioned creating a romantic collection to be treasured and enjoyed by all.”
